About our Farm Park

Galloway Farm Park is a recently renovated, educational farm park, offering not only an educational experience, but also a fun packed day out for all the family. Situated in Moffat, between Edinburgh and Carlisle, this brand new attraction offers a great trip out for young and old, families and people just passing through. We have a free carpark situated at the front of the Farm Park.

Outside there are lots of exciting and unusual animals waiting to meet you and show you their homes and personalities, from foxes to pheasants as well as our mischievous Meerkats. There is also a half mile ramble course where you will find even more of our farm animals in the paddocks, including Reindeer and Highland Cows. You may even see a Woodpecker in the Little Wood.

We also have a garden centre showcasing all our plants, cacti & succlulents, with some also for sale. There is a small pond situated inside the garden centre and if you are lucky you may even spot some fish and frogs!

Do not to fear! If the Great British weather is up to its usual tricks, we have plenty going on indoors, with a small petting zoo with all your farm yard favourites including newly hatched chicks, Rabbits and Guinea Pigs plus sandpits, pedal tractors, a cafeteria and seating areas for refreshments or even tasty meals for your long days out.

Cacti & Succulents

Cacti and succulents are great low maintenance plants to keep at home. We love them here at Galloway Park Farm. Some of the Cacti we keep are Ricktack Cacti, Fairy Castle Cacti, Vebus Fly Trap and Golden Barrel Cacti. Some of the succulents we keep are Aloe Vera, Jade Plant, Echeveria and Snake Plant. .

Petting Zoo

The petting zoo is inside, so it is nice and cosy for all our farmyard favourites. The newly hatched chicks in Farmers Corner are always happy to have some visitors and if you are lucky one of our staff members will let you hold one. The Rabbits and Guinea Pigs love it when you give them a carrot and these are available from the reception when you arrive. We even have a 80yr old Tortoise called Monty that keeps watch over our Petting Zoo, so make sure to say hello if you see him.

Cafe

Our Cafe is actually also a Cat Cafe! We have 9 cats that live at the Farm Park and they are often in the Cafe! So come and say hello, they love attention, so make sure to give them a pet. We have a range of hot and cold food and drinks to eat in and takeaway. We provide Sandwiches and panini's to toasties and jacket potato's.We also have local produce and a array of homemade baked goods.We also have plenty of Vegan and intolerance free options available
