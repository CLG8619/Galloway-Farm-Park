Car Park

Our car park is free of charge and located just as you drive into the farm park. There is also access and spaces for campervans and motorhomes.

Moffat

Moffat is a historic spa town on the River Annan in Dumfries and Galloway, about 17 miles north of Lockerbie. It is also just off the busy A74(M) making it an excellent stopover point for travellers. A large area of Moffat is preserved as a conservation area, ensuring that its architectural and cultural heritage is preserved.

Nearby

There is plenty to do nearby our farm park if you are staying overnight. As well as Moffats busy high street there is also plenty of attractions nearby. Grey Mares Tail walk and waterfall, The Green Frog Gift Shop, Station Park, Moffat Museum, Annandale Way and that is just to name a few!
