Our Attractions

We try to provide a wide range of attractions for everyone to enjoy from our Cat Cafe, Petting Zoo and Play Parks to our Garden Shop.We have a free carpark situated at the front of the Farm Park. Outside there are lots of exciting and unusual animals waiting to meet you and show you their homes and personalities, from llama"s and foxes to pheasants, as well as our mischievous Meerkats. There is also a half mile ramble course where you will find even more of our farm animals in the paddocks, including Reindeer and Highland Cows. You may even see a Woodpecker in the Little Wood. We also have a garden centre showcasing all our plants, cacti & succlulents, with some also for sale. There is a small pond situated inside the garden centre and if you are lucky you may even spot some fish and frogs! Do not to fear! If the Great British weather is up to its usual tricks, we have plenty going on indoors, with a small petting zoo with all your farm yard favourites including newly hatched chicks, Rabbits and Guinea Pigs plus sandpits, pedal tractors, a cafeteria and seating areas for refreshments or even tasty meals for your long days out.Not only do we have pedal tractors and sandpits we alo have a play park!

Play Park

Our Play Park is top of the range and is for ages 4-14yrs of age.There is a small assault course, swings, slide, igloo and see-saw ready for lots of fun!

Pedal Tractors

We have pedal tractors available at the farm park for ages 3-10yrs old, these are a super fun new addition we have at the park, enjoy! .

Cat Cafe

Our Cat Cafe has a range of hot and cold, food and drinks to eat in and and takeaway. The cosy cafe also has a little group of gentle cats who often snuggle up and look forward to a friendly pet from their visitors, be sure to say hello!

About Us

Based in Moffat, we pride ourselves on continously expanding our small farm park to include more educational and agricultural experiances for everyone to learn from and enjoy. To find out more go to our About page.

Our fully qualified team of staff are continuously training to be the best so you know you are in safe hands.
